Private Tours

City tours, day trips, trekking and desert adventures — all customizable.

City Tours (Private)

  • Marrakech City Tour Monuments, souks and hidden gems with a local guide.
  • Fes Guided City Tour Explore the UNESCO medina, crafts and historical landmarks.
  • Essaouira City Tour Coastal charm, port, medina and Atlantic vibes.
  • Ouarzazate & Ait Ben Haddou Kasbah (UNESCO) and desert gateway landscapes.

Day Trips from Marrakech

  • Ourika Valley — Waterfalls, villages and nature.
  • Imlil & Atlas Mountains — Easy hike, Berber culture, panoramic views.
  • Ouzoud Waterfalls — Morocco’s most famous cascades.
  • Essaouira Day Trip — Atlantic coast and free time in the medina.
  • Agafay Desert — Sunset, camel ride, optional dinner.

Atlas Trekking

  • Mount Toubkal Trek – 2 Days — Summit the highest peak in North Africa with mule support.
  • Mount Toubkal Ascent – 3 Days — More acclimatization and deeper Atlas exploration.
  • Berber Villages Trek – 2–3 Days — Traditional villages, tea stops and valley trails.
  • Atlas Valleys Trek – 4 Days — Multiple valleys, passes and authentic landscapes.

Desert Tours

  • Merzouga Desert – 3 Days — Camel trek and night in a desert camp.
  • Sahara Experience – 4 Days — More dunes time + cultural stops.
  • Grand Desert Tour – 5–7 Days — Extended adventure across mountains, kasbahs and dunes.
